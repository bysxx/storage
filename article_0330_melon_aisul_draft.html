What JINRO Melon Soju Actually Tastes Like

First things first: what does it taste like? JINRO Melon Limited Edition is built around the sweet, rounded flavor of melon, balanced with JINRO's signature clean and crisp base. The result is something smooth enough to sip on its own, but light enough that it does not overpower whatever you are eating alongside it.

At 13% ABV, it sits right in line with the rest of the flavored JINRO range. Not too strong, not too light. If you have tried other JINRO flavors and liked the balance between fruitiness and drinkability, this one follows that same approach with melon's naturally mellow sweetness doing the work.

Whether it is a weeknight at home, a rooftop dinner with friends, or a late-night snack run that turns into a proper hangout, JINRO Melon fits the moment without trying too hard.

The Black Label: Why This JINRO Soju Bottle Looks Different

If you are used to the bright, colorful bottles from JINRO's regular flavored lineup, this one will catch your eye for a different reason. JINRO Melon Limited Edition comes in a sleek black label, which is a first for the brand's flavored soju range.
