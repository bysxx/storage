Already enjoying JINRO? Here is a new way to take it further

If JINRO has already been part of your everyday moments—at home, while traveling, or simply during your day-to-day life—this is a chance to turn those moments into something more. Jinro Global Agent is a global supporters program designed for people who enjoy JINRO and want to express that through their own content. If that sounds like you, here is what to know.

Not Your Typical Supporters Program

What makes Jinro Global Agent stand out is how it's built. Rather than a single collaboration, this is a mission-based program where you'll be working alongside JINRO throughout the season. Each month, you'll receive a themed mission tailored to your country, create content around it, and share it on Instagram or TikTok with the hashtag #JINROGlobalAgent.

You'll get to join offline missions, try new flavors before everyone else, and be part of something that goes way beyond a single post.

And the perks don't stop there. Every mission you complete comes with a reward, and if your content performs well, there are additional incentives based on your results. On top of that, standout content could get featured on JINRO's official social channels.

· · ·

Who Can Apply and Where

For this first round, JINRO Global Agent is recruiting in four countries: Japan, Vietnam, the Philippines, and the US. Each country gets its own seasonal missions designed around local culture, so the experience will feel different depending on where you are.

In Japan, that could mean cherry blossom picnics and Golden Week getaways. In the Philippines, think beachside hangs and night market crawls. Vietnam missions will center around the street food scene and nightlife culture. And in the US, expect festival season vibes, backyard BBQs, and summer beach moments.

Whatever season brings to your city, that's your mission. A chance to enjoy JINRO through every moment your country has to offer.

The requirements to apply are simple:

  • You're of legal drinking age in your country
  • You're a local resident of Japan, Vietnam, the Philippines, or the US
  • You have an active Instagram or TikTok account (500+ followers preferred, but not required)
  • You love JINRO and want to share that through your content

You don't need to be an influencer. You don't need a massive following. If you enjoy JINRO and love creating content around food, drinks, and good times, this program was made for you.
