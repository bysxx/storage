JINRO's Toad Just Became a K-Pop Idol — Meet the K-POP Star Toad

JINRO's mascot steps onto the label as a K-pop performer for a summer-only Green Grape edition, complete with figurines, bottle mics, and a bottle-cap message challenge.

The JINRO Toad has a stage name now. For summer 2026, JINRO's signature mascot appears on the label as a full K-pop performer — stage lights, leather jacket, lightstick in hand — on a new limited edition of JINRO Green Grape rolling out across 21 markets. It's called the K-POP Star Toad, and it comes with actual merch.

What is the K-POP Star Toad?

It's a summer limited edition built on JINRO Green Grape, the best-selling flavor in JINRO's overseas fruit soju lineup of six — Grapefruit, Green Grape, Plum, Strawberry, Peach, and Lemon. The soju inside is the Green Grape you already know; the label is where the show happens.

There are two versions. On the green bottle, the Toad works the stage in a black-and-white leather jacket, holding a lightstick up to the crowd. On the pink bottle, a pink Toad takes over with a bow and a glittering black stage jacket. Both are set against an LED wall of concert lights, so the pair sitting side by side reads like a two-member unit mid-performance.

One thing worth being clear about: this is not a collaboration with any specific idol or group. It's JINRO's own mascot reimagined as one — a concept edition, which is part of why it works. The Toad gets to be the star.

Sold in 21 markets, gone after summer

The K-POP Star Toad started rolling out in late June across 21 major markets, including the United States, Japan, and China, and runs through the peak summer season only. Quantities are limited by market, which puts this squarely in collectible territory — the kind of bottle people pick up in pairs so they can keep one of each design.

The merch: figurines and bottle mics

Alongside the bottles, JINRO is releasing two K-POP Star Toad figurines and bottle-shaped microphones as limited-run merch. The figurine leans all the way into the concept — the Toad in full stage outfit, lightstick raised, standing on a base stamped JINRO K-POP EDITION, like a fan-goods figure you'd line up for at a concert pop-up.

Check your bottle cap

The participation piece is the Message Challenge. Each bottle cap has a letter printed inside, and the idea is simple: collect caps, combine the letters into your own message, and post it. It turns the one part of the bottle everyone throws away into the part people photograph — and it gives every round at the table a reason to check under the cap.

JINRO's global K-pop summer

The timing here isn't random. The same summer, JINRO named V of BTS its first-ever global ambassador — the first announcement of its kind for the world's No.1 soju brand. The K-POP Star Toad is the playful side of that same push: bringing JINRO closer to global K-pop culture from both directions, one through a global superstar, one through a toad in a leather jacket.

"Through the synergy between K-pop and the Toad character, we will further strengthen JINRO's mass appeal and global competitiveness," said Hwang Jeong-ho, who leads HiteJinro's overseas business division.

Where to find it
