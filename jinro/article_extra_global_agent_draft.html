JINRO Is Recruiting Secret Agents in 4 Countries. Here's How to Join the Mission.

You've been drinking JINRO at house parties, at BBQs, at late-night convenience store runs, and maybe even on the beach. But what if you could turn all of that into something bigger? What if JINRO actually wanted you to do more of it?

That's exactly the idea behind JINRO Global Agent, a brand-new supporters program launching across four countries this spring. It's not a one-time sponsorship. It's not a paid ad deal. It's a monthly mission-based program where real soju lovers create content their way, on their turf, in their city.

And yes, it's themed like a secret agent operation. Missions, codenames, the whole thing.

So What Exactly Is a JINRO Global Agent?

Think of it as a global crew of JINRO fans who get monthly missions to create content around how they enjoy soju in real life. No scripts. No rigid brand guidelines telling you exactly what to say. Just your actual lifestyle, your local food scene, your go-to hangout spots, and a bottle of JINRO somewhere in the mix.

The program runs on a seasonal basis. Season 1 kicks off in April and runs through June, with 50 agents selected per country. Each agent receives a welcome kit, gets assigned monthly missions tailored to their local culture, and shares the results on Instagram or TikTok using the hashtag #JINROGlobalAgent.

Top-performing content gets featured on JINRO's official social channels. And if your content really takes off, there are bonus rewards based on views and engagement.

· · ·

Where It's Happening: Japan, Vietnam, Philippines, and the US

JINRO Global Agent is launching in four countries, each with its own local mission themes designed around how people actually drink and socialize there.

In Japan, spring means cherry blossom picnics and Golden Week getaways. Agents there will capture soju moments during hanami season, summer BBQs, and festival season later in the year.

In the Philippines, it's all about beating the summer heat. Think beachside hangs, night market crawls, and the kind of laid-back weekend gatherings that soju was practically made for.

In Vietnam, the mission themes center around street food culture and the vibrant nightlife scene. From sidewalk dining in Ho Chi Minh City to night markets and late-night hangouts, agents will document how JINRO fits into the local food-and-drink culture.

And in the US, missions will tap into festival season (yes, Coachella), backyard BBQ culture, and early summer beach vibes across the coasts.

How the Missions Work

Every month, agents receive a local mission based on what's happening in their country at that time. Cherry blossom season in Japan? That's a mission. Rainy season comfort food in Vietnam? Also a mission. The themes are built around real seasonal moments so the content feels natural, not forced.

Agents post their mission content on Instagram or TikTok with the required hashtag and JINRO product visibility. That's it. No 10-page brief. No mandatory talking points. The whole point is that the content should feel like something you'd post anyway, just with a little more intention behind it.

On top of the monthly local missions, there are also global campaign support missions where agents help amplify bigger JINRO campaigns through engagement, story shares, and resharing.

What Agents Get

Let's talk perks. Every selected agent receives:

  • A welcome gift kit when you join
  • Mission rewards for each completed assignment
  • A chance to have your content featured on JINRO's official channels
  • Invitations to exclusive offline brand events in your country
  • Bonus incentives if your content hits major view milestones

The more missions you complete and the better your content performs, the more you earn. It's designed to reward consistency and creativity, not just follower count.

Who Can Apply

JINRO Global Agent is open to anyone who meets a few basic requirements:

  • You're of legal drinking age in your country
  • You're a local resident of Japan, Vietnam, the Philippines, or the US
  • You have an active Instagram or TikTok account (500+ followers preferred, but not required)
  • You genuinely enjoy JINRO and want to share that with your community

You don't need to be an influencer. You don't need a massive following. If you're someone who already enjoys JINRO with friends and likes creating content around food, drinks, and good times, this program was built for people like you.
