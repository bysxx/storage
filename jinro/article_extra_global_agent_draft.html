Wondering if you are eligible for the Jinro Global Agent program?

Jinro Global Agent is open to anyone who genuinely enjoys JINRO and finds fun in creating content. If you like capturing moments, sharing your lifestyle, and expressing your own vibe, you are already a great fit.
