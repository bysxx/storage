What Does JINRO Melon Soju Taste Like?

Let's get to the part everyone actually cares about.

This one leans into a soft, sweet melon flavor — but keeps things balanced. You still get JINRO's signature clean finish, so it doesn't feel heavy or overly sugary. It's smooth, slightly aromatic, and easy to drink whether you're having it on its own or with food.

At 13% ABV, it sits right in line with the rest of JINRO's flavored soju range — light enough for casual drinking, but not watered down.

HiteJinro also noted that this special release was designed to achieve a near-perfect balance that appeals broadly to global tastes, so this melon edition is definitely one to watch.

What Makes This One Stand Out?

Now for the part you'll probably notice first.

If you've seen JINRO's usual flavored soju lineup, you'll notice this one right away. Instead of the typical bright colors, JINRO Melon Limited Edition comes in a black label — a first for the brand's flavored soju range.

The design is simple, minimal, and a bit more elevated. It leans into a premium, limited-edition feel, making it stand out on the shelf without trying too hard.

If you're a longtime JINRO fan, this one might even feel worth keeping.
