JINRO Melon Just Released a Color-Changing Soju Glass

Pour the soju in, and the melon on the glass turns vivid green. Here's the product, the reveal, and where to find it.

JINRO Melon, the black-label limited edition that rolled out across 20+ countries this spring, just got its own soju glass — and it changes color the moment soju hits it.

Image: JINRO Melon's new color-changing soju glass — empty on the left, filled on the right.
What the Glass Does

When the glass is empty, the melon illustration on it sits in a soft, almost faded outline. Pour JINRO Melon in, and that illustration turns into a saturated melon green in seconds. There's no slow gradient — it reads as a single instant.

The change is built around the temperature and contact with the liquid, so every fresh pour resets it. The first cup of the night becomes the visual cue that the round has started, which is exactly the kind of small moment that travels well on Reels and TikTok.

A Quick Refresher on JINRO Melon

If you missed the launch, JINRO Melon is JINRO's seventh fruit liqueur and rolled out from late March 2026 across more than 20 countries, including the U.S., Japan, Vietnam, Australia, and the U.K.

  • ABV: 13%
  • Profile: soft melon aroma, gentle natural sweetness, clean dry finish
  • Bottle: matte black label — a step away from the brighter pastels of the existing fruit soju lineup

The new glass extends that black-label look into the tableware. Black bottle, transparent cup, vivid green reveal — that's the full visual.
