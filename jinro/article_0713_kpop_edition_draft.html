JINRO's Toad Just Made Its K-Pop Debut

JINRO's iconic Toad is stepping into its K-pop era. Meet the JINRO K-POP Edition, a limited summer release that gives JINRO Green Grape a stage-ready new look across 21 countries.

JINRO Green Grape is getting a summer makeover.

The new JINRO K-POP Edition reimagines JINRO's beloved Toad in a K-pop-inspired concept, turning the bottle into something that feels ready for the stage, the table, and your camera roll. The edition began rolling out in late June across 21 countries, including the United States, Japan, and China, bringing a limited seasonal look to one of JINRO's most recognizable fruit soju flavors.

With its playful stage-inspired design, the edition is made for fans of K-culture, summer nights, and bottles that deserve a closer look.

A K-Pop-Inspired Look for JINRO Green Grape

The JINRO K-POP Edition comes in two label designs, each featuring the Toad in a full stage-ready concept.

One label shows the green Toad in a bold performance look, while the other features a pink Toad with her own idol-style attitude. Together, the two bottles feel like a mini two-member debut, complete with concert lights, stage energy, and the bright green grape details that connect back to the flavor inside.

The drink itself is JINRO Green Grape, so the limited edition does not change the flavor. What changes is the mood around it. It is still the crisp, fruity green grape soju people know, now wrapped in a bottle that feels more collectible, more playful, and very ready for summer.

The Detail Hidden Under the Cap

Here is the fun part: when you open a JINRO K-POP Edition bottle, there is a letter printed inside the cap.

It is a small detail, but it makes the bottle feel a little more interactive, especially if you are sharing it with friends. Since this is the K-POP Edition, you can turn the cap letter into a simple table game. Open the bottle, check the letter, and take turns naming a K-pop song, idol group, or artist that starts with that letter.

You can also use the letter as a fun way to show your fan energy online. Find a K-pop song, idol, or artist name that starts with the letter inside your cap, then post it on social media as a small tribute to your favorite.

Or keep it even more personal. Try using the letters to build your own name, a friend's name, or an inside-joke nickname that only your group understands.

It is easy, low-pressure, and exactly the kind of small moment that makes a casual night feel more fun. No complicated rules, no big setup. Just a bottle, a few friends, and whatever K-pop knowledge is already living rent-free in your brain.

Where to find it

The JINRO K-POP Edition began rolling out in late June across 21 countries, including the United States, Japan, and China. Availability may vary by market, and quantities may be limited depending on the region.

If you spot the green or pink bottle at your local Korean grocery store or wherever JINRO fruit soju is sold, that is your window.
