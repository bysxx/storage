You may have seen the teaser. You may have already felt the buzz. Now, it is official: BTS V is JINRO's new global ambassador.

BTS V Is Officially JINRO's New Global Ambassador

JINRO has officially welcomed BTS V as its new global ambassador, opening a new global chapter for the brand.

Known for his global reach and distinctive style, V brings a new presence to JINRO's story. It is a meeting of two names with strong roots in Korean culture: JINRO, with its long-standing place in Korean drinking culture, and V, whose work continues to reach audiences around the world.

V Steps Into the JINRO Story

The story first began on July 9, when V appeared across JINRO's official social channels. The teaser introduced the new partnership and quickly drew attention online.

Now, the main film is out.

In the main film released today, V is officially introduced as JINRO's global ambassador. The film gives audiences a closer look at JINRO's cool and refined mood, with V bringing a calm, natural presence to the scene.

It is the official reveal, but it also feels like the beginning of something bigger: a new way for JINRO to tell its story to global audiences.

Why V?

V is an artist with a strong global reach and a clear personal style, making him a natural fit for JINRO's next chapter. As JINRO continues to connect with consumers around the world, this collaboration brings together a Korean brand with deep heritage and an artist recognized across regions and cultures.

Jung-ho Hwang, Head of Overseas Business at HiteJinro, shared that V naturally represents the brand values and sensibility JINRO pursues. He also noted that through upcoming activities with V, JINRO aims to connect more closely with consumers worldwide while continuing to expand the brand's global presence, building on its leadership and heritage.

In short, this is about more than one film. It is about JINRO staying rooted in Korean culture while meeting global consumers in a way that feels current, cultural, and easy to connect with.
