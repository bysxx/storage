JINRO Just Named Its First-Ever Global Ambassador: V of BTS

The world's best-selling soju now has a global face. Announced in July 2026, the partnership puts V at the front of JINRO's push to bring soju to more people around the world.

The world's best-selling soju just introduced its first global face — and yes, it is V of BTS.

In early July, JINRO named V — Kim Taehyung — as its first-ever global ambassador. The brand is sold in 91 countries, and this is the first time it has put one person at the front of all of them at once.

So What Does That Mean?

V will front JINRO's global marketing and also take part in campaigns in Korea. The idea behind it is simple: make soju something more people around the world feel comfortable picking up, ordering, and sharing.

Two campaign posters came out with the announcement — clean, portrait-style visuals of V with a bottle of JINRO in hand. More is expected to roll out through the summer, so consider this the opening frame.

What JINRO Says

Here is how the brand put it: "V is the person who can best express the brand values and sensibility that JINRO pursues," said Hwang Jung-ho, head of the overseas business division at HiteJinro. "Through our activities with V, we will communicate more closely with consumers around the world and continue expanding the JINRO brand."

Why V Fits

If you follow fashion, you have seen V front global houses like Celine and Cartier. Add one of the largest fandoms in music, and BTS active as a full group again in 2026 with a world tour running — his visibility is at a peak, and it lands exactly where JINRO is headed: global, young, and easy to love.

JINRO's K-Pop Summer

If this feels like part of a bigger moment, that is because it is. This is the same summer JINRO released the K-POP Edition, dressing its Toad mascot as an idol on Green Grape bottles across 21 countries. An idol Toad on the shelf, a BTS member fronting the brand — JINRO's summer is unmistakably K-pop.
