A quick reminder of the program

JINRO Global Agent is a mission-based supporter program built around content creation. Selected agents create reels and social posts each month, with missions designed to show how JINRO fits into local culture, everyday gatherings, and the small scenes people already like sharing.

The official Agent page lists the initial launch markets as the United States, the Philippines, Vietnam, and Japan, with the program built for creators who are active on social media and eligible to appear in alcohol-related content under local rules.
