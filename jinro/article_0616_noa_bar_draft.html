The sound: disco therapy

The short version: bright, danceable pop-disco that still says something. If you want a way in, DIAGNOSIS is her most-streamed track and her 2025 debut album DANCEABLE PROBLEMS is the place to start. She has been releasing steadily, from the RANDOM LIPS project in late 2024 to the album and a run of remixes since, which is part of why she keeps surfacing on new playlists. Because she writes and produces her own work, the catalog reads as one consistent point of view rather than a string of singles.
