If You Do Not Know the Teams, Start with the Colors

NBA teams are not only logos. They are colors. Lakers gold, Celtics green, Knicks orange, Rockets red, Thunder blue. Even if you do not know every player, those colors make the teams easier to remember.

That is the point of this JINRO guide. It is not trying to match every team in the bracket. It is just a quick visual shortcut for the teams whose colors already line up with a JINRO bottle.

If you are a JINRO lover and an NBA lover, this is the easiest way to make the night feel more intentional: let everyone drink the flavor that matches the team they are backing.

How to Make It a JINRO Watch Party

Pick the game first. Then pick the flavor by team color. That is all the setup needs.

  • Lakers vs. Rockets: JINRO Lemon vs. JINRO Strawberry
  • Knicks vs. Hawks: JINRO Peach vs. JINRO Strawberry
  • Thunder vs. Suns: Chamisul Fresh vs. JINRO Grapefruit
  • Celtics vs. 76ers: JINRO Green Grape for the Boston side
  • Cavaliers vs. Raptors: JINRO Plum for the Cleveland side

If your friends are split between teams, put the bottles on opposite sides of the table. If nobody knows who to support yet, let them pick by flavor first and team second. Either way, the game becomes easier to follow.
