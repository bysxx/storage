If You Do Not Know the Teams, Start with the Colors

NBA teams are easy to remember by color: Los Angeles Lakers gold, Boston Celtics green, New York Knicks orange, Houston Rockets red, Oklahoma City Thunder blue. Even if you do not know every player, the colors help you follow who is who.

For a casual watch party, that is enough. Pick the team color first, then match it with the closest JINRO bottle. It keeps the idea simple even if not everyone knows the roster.

If your group is split between teams, let each side choose the flavor that matches the colors they are backing.
