JINRO is bringing its new IU label soju bottles to Southeast Asia. From the limited-edition packaging to where fans can pick one up, here's everything you need to know about JINRO's latest launch with one of Korea's biggest stars.

The Product

Good news for IU fans across Southeast Asia.

This May, JINRO Chamisul is rolling out as a limited-run IU Label Edition — the same Chamisul soju that pairs naturally with food, now wearing a special IU label that fans can keep long after the bottle's empty.

The launch markets are Vietnam and Taiwan, with bottles arriving from the last week of May 2026 and rolling out gradually across each market. As with most JINRO limited editions, once the run sells through, that's it.

Where to Find It

The IU Label Edition will be available through local distribution networks across Vietnam and Taiwan.
